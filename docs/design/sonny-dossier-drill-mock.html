Executive read

CDCP1 is a mechanistically compelling oncology target, over-expressed across several carcinomas and driving metastasis through matriptase-dependent cleavage. The case to keep it on the watchlist rather than commit rests on a single unresolved question: near-ubiquitous normal-tissue expression makes off-target and immunogenicity risk the gating uncertainty. Mechanism and disease association are strong; the clinical pipeline is thin.

Developability risk - significant, not severe
Off-target toxicity and immunogenicity are manageable with a cleavage-selective or conditional binder. A severe liability would force NO-GO; here it holds the verdict at WATCH.
