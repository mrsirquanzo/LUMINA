**Built with Claude Sonnet 4** with deep knowledge of biotech markets, epidemiology, competitive intelligence, and commercial strategy.

## Example Questions

Here are specific questions the agent can answer:

### Market Sizing & Opportunity
- "Size the market opportunity for a novel obesity drug in the US and EU"
- "Calculate the addressable patient population for a CAR-T therapy in relapsed/refractory DLBCL"
- "Analyze the epidemiology and market size for Duchenne muscular dystrophy"
- "What's the total addressable market for an Alzheimer's disease therapy?"

### Competitive Analysis
- "Map the competitive landscape for checkpoint inhibitors in non-small cell lung cancer"
- "Compare clinical profiles of leading BTK inhibitors for chronic lymphocytic leukemia"
- "Identify competitive positioning opportunities for a novel NASH therapy"
- "Who are the key competitors in the gene therapy space for hemophilia A?"

### Pricing & Market Access
- "Benchmark pricing for rare disease therapies. What's a defensible price point for our orphan drug?"
- "Analyze payer coverage and reimbursement dynamics for CAR-T therapies"
- "Design a market access strategy for a high-cost gene therapy ($2M+ per patient)"
- "What pricing premium can we command with breakthrough efficacy data?"

### Commercial Strategy & Forecasting
- "Forecast peak sales for a best-in-class oral GLP-1 agonist for obesity"
- "Build a 10-year revenue model for our cell therapy product"
- "Estimate market penetration rate for a novel Alzheimer's drug with accelerated approval"
- "What's the realistic timeline to reach $1B in annual sales?"
