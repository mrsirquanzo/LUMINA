## Overview

AI-powered market research analyst for biotech commercial intelligence, competitive analysis, and opportunity assessment. Get strategic insights on market sizing, pricing, payer dynamics, and revenue forecasting with real-time M&A tracking. Built with **Perplexity Sonar Pro** featuring live market data integration.

**Data Sources:** 📰 Market Data APIs • 📊 Industry News Sources • 💼 Business Intelligence
