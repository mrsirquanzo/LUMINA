## Overview

The Financial Intelligence Agent is an AI-powered assistant that analyzes SEC filings, financial models, and biotech company financials. Upload 10-K/10-Q PDFs, Excel models, earnings presentations, or provide investor relations URLs to get instant analysis of burn rate, runway, valuations, and investment risks.

**Built with Google Gemini 2.0 Flash** featuring large context windows, fast processing, and vision capabilities for analyzing charts and financial visualizations.

## Example Questions

Here are specific questions the agent can answer when you upload relevant financial documents:

### Financial Statement Analysis
- "What's the quarterly burn rate and estimated cash runway?"
- "Extract and analyze all revenue line items from this 10-K"
- "What are the key risk factors mentioned in the MD&A section?"
- "Compare Q3 vs Q4 operating expenses—where did spending increase?"

### Valuation & Investment Thesis
- "Perform a DCF valuation with 12% discount rate and these revenue assumptions"
- "What comparable companies should be used for this gene therapy developer?"
- "Assess the investment thesis for this Phase 2 oncology company"
- "What's a fair valuation for this pre-revenue platform company?"

### Deal & Transaction Analysis
- "Analyze the terms of this $2B acquisition—is it favorable for the buyer?"
- "What's the NPV of this licensing deal with 8% royalty on $500M peak sales?"
- "Compare this M&A multiple to recent biotech transactions"
- "Assess the financial impact of this partnership announcement"

### Risk Assessment
- "What are the top 3 financial risks for this company?"
- "Analyze the debt covenants and refinancing risks"
- "How does the burn rate compare to peer companies?"
- "What's the probability of needing additional financing in the next 18 months?"

## Supported Formats

**Documents**
- PDF (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, S-1, earnings presentations)
- Excel/CSV (financial models, pro forma financials, cap tables)
- Plain text (press releases, investor updates)

**Images**
- Charts and graphs from investor presentations
- Financial visualizations and dashboards

**Web Content**
- SEC EDGAR filing URLs
- Company investor relations pages
- Deal announcement press releases

**Limits**: Up to 5 files per query, 10MB max file size

---

## Key Features

**Citation Tracking** ✅
Every financial metric and insight references its source document and page number. Citations appear inline as `[Source: 10-K FY2024, Page 42]`.

**Multi-Document Analysis**
Upload multiple filings (e.g., 10-K + 10-Q) to analyze trends over time or compare financial performance across periods.

**Vision-Enabled**
Claude's vision API analyzes financial charts, revenue breakdowns, and waterfall charts directly from images or presentation slides.

**Calculation Transparency**
All valuation calculations show their work—assumptions, formulas, and sensitivity analyses are explained step-by-step.
