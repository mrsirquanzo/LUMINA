**Built with Claude Sonnet 4** with deep knowledge of regulatory guidance documents and approval pathways.

## Example Questions

Here are specific questions the agent can answer:

### Regulatory Pathway Strategy
- "What is the optimal regulatory pathway for a novel CAR-T therapy? Should we pursue Breakthrough Therapy designation?"
- "Design a regulatory strategy for a Phase 2 Alzheimer's drug with accelerated approval potential"
- "Compare the IND vs. NDA pathway for a 505(b)(2) application"
- "What are the requirements for RMAT designation for our cell therapy product?"

### Timeline & Planning
- "Estimate the timeline from IND to BLA approval for a gene therapy"
- "What are the key milestones and FDA interactions for orphan drug development?"
- "Plan the regulatory meetings needed (Pre-IND, EOP2, Pre-BLA) and their timing"
- "How long does Priority Review take and what triggers FDA to grant it?"

### CMC & Manufacturing
- "What CMC data is required for IND filing vs. BLA filing?"
- "Assess manufacturing requirements for autologous vs. allogeneic CAR-T"
- "What comparability protocols are needed if we change our manufacturing process?"
- "Explain stability testing requirements for biologics under ICH guidelines"

### Compliance & Risk
- "What are common causes of clinical holds for cell therapies?"
- "Identify potential FDA concerns for our combination product"
- "Design a REMS program for a drug with serious safety risks"
- "What post-approval commitments are required for accelerated approval?"

## Key Features

**Guidance Document Citations** ✅
Every recommendation references specific FDA guidance documents, CFR sections, or ICH guidelines with proper citations.

**Multi-Modality Expertise**
Covers regulatory requirements across small molecules, biologics, cell therapies, gene therapies, and combination products.

**Global Regulatory Knowledge**
Understands FDA (US), EMA (EU), PMDA (Japan), and NMPA (China) regulatory frameworks and harmonization initiatives.

**Accelerated Program Expertise**
Expert guidance on Fast Track, Breakthrough Therapy, RMAT, Accelerated Approval, and Priority Review programs.
